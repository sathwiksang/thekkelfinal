Biography

About Dr. Sunny Ezhumattoor

A life dedicated to biblical scholarship and theological education

Dr. Sunny Ezhumattoor is a distinguished biblical scholar and educator with decades of experience in theological research and Christian ministry. His profound commitment to biblical truth has shaped his life's work, resulting in an extensive collection of theological writings that address critical spiritual and doctrinal issues facing the contemporary church.

As a prolific author, Dr. Ezhumattoor has dedicated himself to producing scholarly yet accessible works that bridge the gap between academic theology and practical Christian living. His books, including "Holy Spirit vs Kundalini Spirit," "Marriage and Morality," and "The Science of Biblical Interpretation," reflect his deep understanding of Scripture and his desire to equip believers with sound biblical knowledge.

Dr. Ezhumattoor's ministry extends beyond his written works through personalized online biblical education, where he shares his wealth of knowledge with students seeking to deepen their understanding of Scripture. His teaching approach emphasizes careful exegesis, historical context, and practical application, ensuring that students not only learn biblical content but also develop the skills necessary to study and interpret Scripture independently.
